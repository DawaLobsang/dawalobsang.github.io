SPACE INVADERS
The world is under attack. You have to protect the planet from the aliens. Use "a" key to move left. Use "d" key to move right. Use "w" key to shot. Press "P" to start the level. You can't beat my game :). If you reach a certain score, the aliens will reset to the top, but their movements will get more random. Have fun and see how far you can get. BTW the aliens can teleport. HF!
